Our Services

Interior Design

At Idea Decor, we offer a complete solution for interior design and execution, encompassing bedrooms, majlis, living rooms, dining halls, and stair rooms, providing our clients with functional and aesthetically pleasing living and working spaces. Our attention to detail and expertise ensure a seamless and hassle-free experience for our clients.

Extra Low Voltage System

Idea Decor offers comprehensive solutions for CCTV systems, door access control, gate and barrier systems, and video and audio intercoms, providing our clients with enhanced security and control over their residential or commercial properties. With our expertise and attention to detail, we ensure a seamless and efficient operation of all security systems.

Home Automation System

Our home automation system offers comprehensive control over lighting, AC, music, and sensor systems, as well as Azan, providing a seamless and personalized experience for our clients. With our expertise and attention to detail, we ensure maximum convenience and comfort in all aspects of our clients' living spaces.. Our expertise and attention to detail ensure maximum comfort and convenience for our clients.

Guest Room Management System

Our GRMS (Guest Room Management System) provides a comprehensive solution for managing guest room operations, including DND (Do Not Disturb) ,MUR (Make Up Room), lighting control, AC control, and various types of sensors. With our GRMS, guests can enjoy a seamless and personalized experience, while hotel staff can efficiently manage room operations, ensuring maximum comfort and convenience for guests.

Civil Works

Idea Decor provides a range of comprehensive civil works services, including block works, plastering works, flooring works, painting works, waterproofing works, and false ceiling works, ensuring the highest standards of workmanship and quality for every project. With our attention to detail and commitment to excellence, we deliver customized and cost-effective solutions that cater to our clients' unique requirements and preferences.

Transforming Spaces, Enhancing Lives

Welcome to Idea Decor, a premier interior fit out company located in the heart of Dubai. Established in 2014, we have been providing exceptional interior fit-out services for a decade, and have gained a reputation for delivering high-quality projects that exceed our clients' expectations.

At Idea Decor, we believe that every space has its own unique identity, and our aim is to create a design that reflects the personality and preferences of our clients. With our team of experienced professionals and skilled craftsmen, we have successfully completed a diverse range of interior fit-out projects, including commercial spaces, residential villas, restaurants, and hotels.

Our team at Idea Decor works closely with clients to understand their needs and requirements, and provides customized solutions that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. We take pride in our attention to detail, and every aspect of the project, from conceptualization to execution, is meticulously planned and executed.

We understand the importance of deadlines, and our team works tirelessly to ensure that each project is completed on time and within budget. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has helped us build long-term relationships with our clients, and we take pride in our reputation as a reliable and trusted interior fit-out company in Dubai.

At Idea Decor, we are passionate about what we do, and our aim is to create spaces that inspire and delight. Whether you are looking to transform your home, office or restaurant, our team is here to bring your vision to life. Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can help you with your next interior fit-out project.
